Our Journey

A Decade of Growth & Innovation

2014

The Beginning

VNX Digital was founded in Chennai with a simple vision — to help local businesses grow online. Started with a small team of 3 passionate marketers.

2016

First 100 Clients

Crossed our first major milestone of 100 happy clients. Expanded our team and launched our SEO and Paid Ads division to meet growing demand.

2018

Social Media Boom

Launched our full-fledged Social Media Marketing service. Helped 50+ brands go viral in Tamil Nadu and across India with creative campaigns.

2020

National Recognition

Expanded services to pan-India clients. Won multiple industry awards for excellence in digital marketing during the challenging COVID era.

2022

Email & Automation

Launched advanced email marketing and marketing automation services. Helped clients build automated funnels generating passive revenue streams.

2024

10 Years Strong

Celebrated 10 years of excellence with 500+ clients, 1200+ campaigns, and a team of 25+ digital marketing experts. The best is yet to come!
